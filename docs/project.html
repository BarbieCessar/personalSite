Data Analytics Projects:

This is a project that I did for my Social Media Data Analytics class. I utilized NodeXL Pro to perform a social media network analysis on UPMC’s Twitter account. Based on my findings, I reported an effective marketing strategy for their Strategic Communications Office and Management Team.
